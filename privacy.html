PRIVACY POLICY

Your data stays yours.

Zero collection. Zero network access. Zero analytics. Everything lives on your iPad and nowhere else.

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Overview

Armored Vault is designed with privacy as its core principle. The app operates entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.

Data Collection

Armored Vault collects no data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are gathered
  • No crash reports are sent automatically
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are employed
  • No account creation is required

Network Activity

Armored Vault makes zero network connections. The app does not communicate with any server, API, or external service. All encryption, decryption, and file storage happens entirely on your device.

On-Device Data

All files you import or create within the app are encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored locally on your iPad. Your passphrase is hashed and stored in the iOS Keychain — it is never transmitted or stored in plain text. No data leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Camera Access

The app requests camera access for two optional features: capturing photos and videos directly into the vault, and the break-in detection feature. All captured images remain on your device, encrypted within the app's secure storage. No images are ever uploaded or transmitted.

Face ID & Device Authentication

If you enable Face ID for unlock, authentication is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on your device. The app also uses Apple's device-owner authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode) to gate the Vault Reset Zone, which is the sanctioned clean-slate reset path for forgotten passphrases. No biometric data is accessed or stored by Armored Vault in either case — all evaluation happens within iOS.

Third-Party Services

Armored Vault uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, ad networks, or cloud services of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
